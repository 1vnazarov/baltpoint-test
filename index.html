9.1

\[\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt[5]{3-2x}} = \int (3-2x)^{-\frac{1}{5}} \, dx\]

Пусть:

\[t = 3 - 2x\]
\[dt = -2 \, dx \quad \Rightarrow \quad dx = -\frac{dt}{2}\]
\[\int (3-2x)^{-\frac{1}{5}} \, dx = \int t^{-\frac{1}{5}} \cdot \left(-\frac{dt}{2}\right) = -\frac{1}{2} \int t^{-\frac{1}{5}} \, dt\]
\[\int t^{n} \, dt = \frac{t^{n+1}}{n+1} + C, \quad n \neq -1\]

При \(n = -\frac{1}{5}\):

\[-\frac{1}{2} \int t^{-\frac{1}{5}} \, dt = -\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{t^{-\frac{1}{5}+1}}{-\frac{1}{5}+1} + C = -\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{t^{\frac{4}{5}}}{\frac{4}{5}} + C\]
\[-\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{t^{\frac{4}{5}}}{\frac{4}{5}} = -\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{5}{4} \cdot t^{\frac{4}{5}} = -\frac{5}{8} t^{\frac{4}{5}}\]

Выполним обратную замену \(t = 3 - 2x\):

\[-\frac{5}{8} t^{\frac{4}{5}} + C = -\frac{5}{8} (3-2x)^{\frac{4}{5}} + C\]
\[-\frac{5}{8} (3-2x)^{\frac{4}{5}} + C = -\frac{5}{8} \sqrt[5]{(3-2x)^{4}} + C\]

Проверка: Продифференцируем полученный результат:

\[\frac{d}{dx}\left[-\frac{5}{8} (3-2x)^{\frac{4}{5}}\right] = -\frac{5}{8} \cdot \frac{4}{5}(3-2x)^{-\frac{1}{5}} \cdot (-2) = \frac{1}{2} \cdot 4 \cdot (3-2x)^{-\frac{1}{5}} = (3-2x)^{-\frac{1}{5}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt[5]{3-2x}}\]

Ответ: \(\displaystyle -\frac{5}{8} \sqrt[5]{(3-2x)^{4}} + C\)
